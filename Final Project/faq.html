FAQ's


From our Customers


What is Tapioca Boba?

Tapioca boba come from the cassava root starch, brown sugar and caramel; they have a chewy gummy texture.

What are Jellies made from?

Our tea jellies are made from fresh produce such as strawberries, mango, and aloe vera

What is Popping Boba?

Popping boba look like tapioca boba but instead have juice in them and will pop in your mouth. They are really tasty and a great alternative to tapioca or jellies, and are vegan friendly.

Do I need to add Boba in my Bubble Tea?

Of course not, it is entirely optional. You are in charge of creating your masterpiece, and we simply compose them for you

Are your products vegan friendly?

Yes. All of our products are suitable for vegans.

What's the Difference Between Green and Black Tea?

Both come from the same plant but go through different processing. The big difference is black tea has more caffeine.
