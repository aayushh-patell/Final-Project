History and Products


Bubble tea began life in Taiwan in the 1980’s, before spreading around the countries of East Asia and it wasn’t long before people began taking their favourite snack drink to Australia, the USA and here in Canada Many people think the little balls at the bottom, often called boba, are the origin of the name 'bubble tea', but in fact it comes from the frothy little bubbles which are made when we shake the drink to mix it all up to perfection.


In addition to our large selection of teas, we also offer a lovely selection of ice-creams. Ranging from a simple, natural vanilla to a grand ube ice-cream with a hint of edible gold, we have it all.
